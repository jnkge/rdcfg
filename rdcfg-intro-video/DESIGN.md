# rdcfg Intro Video Design

## Style Prompt

A crisp technical product walkthrough for a Windows CLI. The video should feel like a reliable terminal tool becoming effortless: deep PowerShell navy, precise panels, luminous success green, and restrained motion. Screenshots are the hero material; typography and accents should guide attention without covering the terminal content.

## Colors

- Background: `#07192f` deep terminal navy
- Panel: `#0b2440` blue-black glass panel
- Foreground: `#f2f6ee` warm near-white
- Muted: `#8eb2cc` cool secondary text
- Accent: `#63f08a` CLI success green
- Secondary accent: `#ffce6a` warm command highlight

## Typography

- Chinese/UI copy: `"Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif`
- Commands and metadata: `"Cascadia Code", "JetBrains Mono", monospace`
- Weight contrast: 800-900 for headings, 300-400 for supporting copy.

## Motion

- Medium-energy technical explainer.
- Entrances should be staggered and directional: text builds first, screenshot settles second, accent rails and callouts follow.
- Scene changes use a consistent scan/wipe transition with vertical blocks, like a terminal repaint.
- Screenshots get subtle scale and pan only; preserve readability.

## What NOT To Do

- Do not use purple/cyan generic gradients.
- Do not cover important terminal choices with large captions.
- Do not center everything as a static slide deck.
- Do not use jump cuts between screenshots.
- Do not shrink screenshots so far that CLI text becomes unreadable.
